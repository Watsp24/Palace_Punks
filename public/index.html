The Vibe

That’s how David describes Palace Punks as a DJ. Never afraid to go too deep, the emotive musical selections are the crowds own therapy. Having grown up on his father’s eclectic combination of funk music (George Clinton, Roger Troutman), European electronica (Kraftwerk, Tangerine Dream), BoomBap 80s music (Depeche Mode, Gary Numan)… all which became infused with his own love for both hip-hop and video game music, Palace Punks ended up with quite a mixed spectrum of musical influences. Today, this translates into sets where the DJ’s pick apart and infuses together all the pieces of this music that they loves best: beautiful synth work, ethereal backdrops, brooding basslines, and occasional vocal hooks… all on top of a pronounced foot-shuffling house beat.
